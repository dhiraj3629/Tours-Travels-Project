Popular Destinations

Explore the gems of Maharashtra with us

Ellora Caves

One of the largest rock-cut monastery-temple cave complexes in the world, featuring Buddhist, Hindu and Jain monuments.

Ajanta Caves

Ancient Buddhist cave monuments dating from the 2nd century BCE, famous for their masterpieces of Buddhist religious art.

Mahabaleshwar

A vast plateau bound by valleys on all sides, known for its captivating strawberries, rivers, and magnificent peaks.

Lonavala

A hill station surrounded by green valleys, famous for the Karla Caves and the Bhushi Dam.

Panchgani

Renowned for its tableland, a huge volcanic plateau, offering spectacular views of the coastal plains.

Alibaug

A coastal town known for its beaches and the historic Kolaba Fort.
